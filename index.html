Our Mission

We are a registered empowerment initiative partnered with companies in the blockchain space, including Trust Wallet and Exodus Wallet, offering accessible digital finance solutions worldwide. Our mission, backed by Elon Musk’s innovation vision, is to reduce global financial instability by helping people earn ownership shares in renewable technology projects, crypto investments, and electric vehicle manufacturing.

How It Works

1. Register

Sign up using your Bitcoin public wallet address. No private keys or passwords required.

2. Verify Wallet

Your public wallet address is verified and linked to our smart contract system.

3. Receive Empowerment Balance

Once verified, your account becomes eligible for digital asset allocation in stablecoins (USDT/BUSD).

4. Withdraw Funds

After purchasing a WTD Card, you can withdraw weekly funds linked to your wallet.
